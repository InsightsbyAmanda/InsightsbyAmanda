Professional Profile

I am a Data Analyst and Business Intelligence professional with a proven track record of delivering end-to-end data solutions that create measurable organisational impact. My work is defined by the ability to take complex, multi-source datasets and transform them into coherent, decision-ready intelligence from automated ETL pipelines to dynamic Power BI dashboards deployed at enterprise scale.

My technical depth spans advanced DAX, Power Query, dimensional modeling (star and snowflake schemas), Slowly Changing Dimensions, and Python analytics (Pandas, NumPy, Plotly, NetworkX). Beyond the technical delivery, I bring a problem solving mindset, understanding business objectives first then designing analytics that drive measurable outcomes.

What distinguishes my approach is the bridge I build between technical execution and executive communication. I translate data into narratives that resonate with decision-makers, and I design systems that scale. Currently pursuing a Master's of Business Analytics and Artificial Intelligence (MBAI) at Ontario Tech University, I am deepening expertise at the intersection of advanced analytics, data engineering, AI, and strategic business leadership.
