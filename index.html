About

Turning complexity into clarity —
one dataset at a time.

I am a data analyst with over six years of progressive experience delivering end-to-end analytics solutions across public sector, real estate, and financial services. My career has been defined by the ability to translate raw, complex data into clear, decision-grade insights — from designing ETL pipelines and dimensional models to building enterprise Power BI dashboards serving thousands of stakeholders.

My technical foundation spans advanced SQL, Python, Power BI, and SSIS, underpinned by deep expertise in data warehousing principles including star and snowflake schemas, slowly changing dimensions, and optimized query performance. I take pride not just in building systems that work, but in building systems that scale — reducing refresh times from minutes to seconds and cutting manual processing by 60%.

Beyond the technical, I bring a consulting mindset: understanding business objectives first, then designing analytics that drive measurable outcomes. At Lincolnshire County Council, my analysis directly informed over £2 million in resource allocation decisions affecting thousands of children and families across the region.

Currently expanding my expertise through a Master of Business Analytics and Artificial Intelligence at Ontario Tech University, I am focused on the intersection of advanced analytics, AI-powered decision-making, and real-world business impact.
